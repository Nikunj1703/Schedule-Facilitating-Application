Update Preferences

One of the sources of information for making teaching assignments is the attached form indicating which courses you are interested in teaching, either now or in the future. In order to better understand your teaching interests and abilities, I ask that you to take the time to fill out this form.

While course offerings and instructor availability change, we will not always be able to accommodate your preferences. However, we will take them into consideration as much as possible.

Tenure-line faculty should indicate core courses that they would be comfortable teaching as well as 300 and 400 level courses that are part of the major in their area â i.e. Computer Science faculty should give special attention to indicating their interests and abilities for the courses in the CS program. If you are interested in teaching a topics class (either graduate or undergraduate) in the next few semesters, please give a brief description of the topic at the end of the form. Tenure-line faculty need to specify at least 6 courses that they would be willing to teach if needed.
